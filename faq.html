Frequently Asked Questions

What is Client?

Client is a modpack based from CheatBreaker, with way more features added!

Is it safe & free?

Yes! Client is safe and completely free to download.

How do you get cosmetics on the client?

By being active in our Discord. In doing so you will level up, and the higher the level, the more rewards you'll get!

How can I request for something to be added to Client?

You may suggest new features in our Discord by creating a ticket!

Why should I switch from another client to Client?

Client has more features than the mainstream clients, we offer a clean and less toxic playing environment. Client has some original features such as options for rounded module backgrounds, and even add things around the text!

Does Client boost frames?

Client will often more than double your frames in game!

How often does Client get updated?

Client is updated everyday, and there are hardly any bugs released in each update.
